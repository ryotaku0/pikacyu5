Pikachu are one of the most well-known varieties of Pokémon,
largely because Pikachu is a central character in the Pokémon anime series.
Pikachu is a major character of the Pokémon franchise as well as its mascot,
and has become an icon of Japanese pop culture in recent years.
It is one of the major mascots for Nintendo.
